// FAQ

QUESTIONS PEOPLE YELLED AT US

Answered calmly, which felt like a betrayal of the brand, but here we are.

Is it really free?

Yes. They're HTML files. Charging you would require building a checkout page, and we'd rather build another boss. Priorities.

Why is Rita a pentagon?

Circles are prey, squares are furniture, triangles are villains. Five sides is exactly enough edges to mean business without joining the enemy team. This is settled geometry.

Why doesn't V1 or the Boss Pack have music?

V1 is the quiet one — the silence is load-bearing. The Boss Pack is ink; ink doesn't need a soundtrack. If you want the band, v2 has an entire generative engine that only plays for winners. Go be a winner.

Do my scores sync to the cloud?

They sync to localStorage, also known as "your computer," also known as where your data should have been this whole time.

Will there be more games?

Rita has died approximately 40,000 times in QA and shows no signs of stopping. Draw your own conclusions.

What's your roadmap?

Forward, at 60 frames per second, with occasional hit-stop for dramatic effect.
